Target Audience

Our chamber of commerce caters to the following target audience: Local business owners and entrepreneurs Professionals seeking networking opportunities Community members interested in supporting local businesses

Personels

First Personel : Esi Atta Esi is a small business owner running a store. She is looking for ways to connect with other local business owners and promote her store to a wider audience.

Second Personel : Nana Yaw Yaw is a young busines man . He is interested in networking events and mentorship opportunities to expand and grow his career.

Scenarios

  1. Esi wants to attend a chamber networking event to meet other business owners and potentially collaborate on marketing initiatives.
  2. Yaw is looking for a mentor in the industry and wants to find out if the chamber offers a mentorship program.
  3. A local resident wants to find a directory of all the chamber member businesses to support local shopping.
  4. Esi is interested in the chamber's resources for improving social media marketing strategies.
  5. Yaw wants to learn about upcoming workshops or seminars organized by the chamber that can help him enhance his work.
